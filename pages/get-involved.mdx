# Get Involved

We're looking for public goods maxis to help us build and launch PGN. If that is
you, there are two ways you can join the community and get involved:

1. Join the [Telegram community](https://t.me/+9_ZHRaz7nmJkMTIx) if you're interested in building on PGN and
   helping us get core infrastructure set up on the protocol.
2. If you are interested in deploying on PGN, or joining the Alliance, please
  reach out to pgn@gitcoin.co.
