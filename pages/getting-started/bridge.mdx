# Bridging Funds from Sepolia

Bridging funds from Sepolia to Sepolia PGN is easy using the [PGN
Bridge](https://pgn-bridge.vercel.app/bridge).

Connect your wallet by clicking the button that says "Connect Wallet".

![](/bridging/start-screen.png)

Enter the amount you want to bridge into the "Amount" field and click the
"Deposit" button. This will open Metamask and ask you to confirm the
transaction.

![](/bridging/deposit.png)

Bridging can take a few minutes. You'll see a message that says "Deposit
Successful!" when your deposit finishes and your funds are bridged.

![](/bridging/success.png)
