## Prerequisite

Before adding PGN's testnet to your wallet, you need to have testnets enabled
generally. You can do this by going to Settings > Advanced > Show test networks

![](/testnet-settings.png)

## Adding Sepolia PGN to Metamask

Adding Sepolia PGN to Metamask is easy. These next few steps will walk you
through it and, at the end of this section, you'll be interacting with PGN
testnet through Metamask.

Start by opening Metamask and selecting the network dropdown menu.

![](/add-network.png)

There's a button at the bottom of this menu with the text "Add network" - click
this button. You'll then be taken to a screen where you can search for common
networks.

At the bottom of this screen, there is a link with the text, "Add a network
manually." Click this link. You'll be taken to a new screen with a form for
adding a network:

Fill out this form with the data for Sepolia PGN. This information is in the
table above.

![](/manually-add-network.png)

Click the "Save" button to finish adding Sepolia PGN to Metamask.
